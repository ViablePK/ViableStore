Order Tracking

Welcome to your order tracking page where you can find out the status of your ordered items.

How It Works

Enter your order number and the e-mail address with which you placed the order. Click 'Check Now' to know the status of your order.
If you do not know your order number, check your order confirmation email or SMS. If you signed in from Facebook on Daraz, enter your e-mail address used for Facebook.

Is my order still there?

If you were able to complete your order on Daraz, be assured, your order is under processing. We might not call you and will directly start processing order if you have paid online or if you have shopped from Daraz previously.

Delivery Guidelines

Please have the exact amount ready upon delivery if your payment method is Cash On Delivery. The amount to be paid will be stated on the invoice of your delivered package. No extra charges are applied.
